Every world begins small.

Not with thunder.

Not with prophecy.

But with a notebook.

A stain of coffee.

A single question written in the margin:

What happens after the legends die?

I did not want heroes.

Statues do not bleed.

I wanted survivors.

Men who laugh too loud.
Women who smile like daggers.
Crews held together by debt, not destiny.
